Hibalag Cup 2025: Ultimate Frisbee

   On Sunday, August 1, 2025, from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM, I attended a frisbee tournament at the Filomeno Cimafranca Ballfield as an attendee. My objective was to simply watch the tournament, but it became a more interesting experience than I expected. I stayed at the center of the field with the Red Cross helpers as one of my friends is part of the group. I was observing the players as they competed with each other. It was my first time watching an actual frisbee tournament, since the sport is quite uncommon in my previous campus in Cebu.

   My initial thought was that frisbee was just a casual game of throwing and catching, but I soon realized that it takes skill, communication, and quick decision-making. I was impressed by how organized and disciplined the teams were, as well as how they showed respect toward one another. The most fulfilling part of the experience was witnessing the players' sportsmanship and unity.

   The event gave me something new to watch, I usually just see volleyball or basketball tournaments in Cebu. This gave me a new perspective on how broad sports can be. It reminded me that trying something unfamiliar can lead to new learning and appreciation. One day, I would like to learn how to play frisbee myself so I could play with some of my friends in Tayasan, just a small game though, I dont have the same stamina like an athlete.

   Because of this activity, I am more open to exploring new things. Especially new hobbies and stuff. I want to appreciate the effort and the joy of learning something new.

CCS: Chess Tournament

FPBC: Billiard Tournament

The event aimed to do a friendly competition among player in CS. As part of my role, I was responsible for checking attendance, arranging player matchups, and ensuring the playing area remained organized. Having prior experience in event management, I wanted to make sure that the tournament would run smoothly.

Small task can be very crucial, especially when tracking there scores as it does helped maintain the flow of the event. It was quite fun to see how there are some players that are trying their best to actually learn while playing the game. It was quite rewarding on how players enjoyed themselves all throughout the game.

   This experience reminded me that even with my prior experience, there is always something new to learn from others. Every experience I have had made me realize the importance of recognizing the efforts of every person who made these events possible. I have also learned how crucial it is to be more observant when overseeing a competition.

   Because of this activty, I am more confident on my abilities to organize events. I am proud of how I contributed to the success of the event. I have come to be motivated to do better and improve my skills in doing more events like this.
