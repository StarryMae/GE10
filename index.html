About Me: My WPE Online Journal

Welcome to My WPE Online Journal, my name is Elsa Mae A. Calumba, 22 years old, studying Computer Studies. This is a personal space where I reflect on my experiences, learning, and growth from the Five Cs in Silliman University.

Through this journal, I aim to document how each of these core values shape my journey as a student. It is a space where I connect my academic experiences with my personal insights, allowing me to understand more deeply what it means to live out Whole-Personl Education in everyday life.
